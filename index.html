How it works

The evaluation engine
that tells you the truth.

01

Describe your project and organization

Tell Marginova your sector, country, org type and what you're trying to fund. In any of 40+ languages.

02

The system evaluates your eligibility

Real-time matching against 200+ verified programs. Scores are calculated against eligibility criteria, regional fit, competition level and deadline proximity.

03

You receive a clear decision

TOP 3 results — each labeled APPLY, CONDITIONAL, or BACKUP — with a probability of success, specific risks, and one concrete action you can take today.

04

Build the application draft

Select a program. Marginova generates Abstract, LFM, Budget Draft, Risk Matrix and Submission Checklist. Export as PDF.
