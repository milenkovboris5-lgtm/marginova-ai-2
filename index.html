Match signals — not scores

Instead of a percentage, Marginova shows you specific reasons why a program may be relevant: sector keywords found, country explicitly listed, org type mentioned in eligibility.

⚠️

Risk factors — what to verify

Before you invest time in an application, know what to check: country eligibility gaps, budget mismatches, tight deadlines, global competition levels.

🗄️

Live database — not web scraping

Programs are verified, structured, and updated automatically every week. Web results are used only when the database has fewer than 3 results for your search.

🌍

You choose — Marginova informs

No YES/NO decisions, no APPLY/BACKUP labels. The donor is the one who decides eligibility. Marginova gives you the information to make your own informed choice.
