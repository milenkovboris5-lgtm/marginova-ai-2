Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Marginova.AI

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Marginova.AI, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

Marginova.AI is an AI-powered platform offering specialized AI assistants for personal growth, education, wellness and productivity. We are operated as an individual business registered in North Macedonia.

Contact: contact@marginova.ai

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account data: Email address and encrypted password when you register
  • Conversation data: Messages you send to our AI assistants, stored to enable memory features
  • Usage data: XP points, level progress, and activity within the platform
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information for security and rate limiting
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Paddle — we never see or store your card details

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our AI assistant services
  • To enable conversation memory between sessions
  • To manage your account, XP and subscription
  • To prevent abuse and enforce usage limits
  • To communicate important updates about the service

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database provider. Conversations are stored encrypted and access is restricted to your account only. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. AI Conversations

Messages sent to our AI assistants are processed by Groq API (powered by Llama AI models). These conversations may be used to improve AI model performance in anonymized form. We recommend not sharing sensitive personal, financial or medical information in conversations.

Important: Our AI assistants provide general informational content only. They are not licensed medical professionals, therapists, lawyers or financial advisors. Always consult a qualified professional for specific advice.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect functionality.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@marginova.ai

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Conversation history older than 100 messages per assistant is automatically deleted. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Groq — AI model processing (groq.com/privacy)
  • Vercel — hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Paddle — payment processing (paddle.com/legal/privacy)

10. Children's Privacy

Marginova.AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: contact@marginova.ai
