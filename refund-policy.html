Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Marginova.AI

✅ We want you to be satisfied. If you are not happy with your Pro subscription, we offer a straightforward refund process. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Free Plan

The free plan is completely free — no payment, no refund needed. You can use 50 messages per day at no cost, forever.

2. Pro Subscription — 14-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to Marginova.AI Pro (monthly or annual) and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

Conditions for refund eligibility:

  • Request submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date
  • First-time purchase only (not applicable to renewals)
  • Account has not been used to abuse the service

3. Subscription Renewals

Monthly and annual subscriptions renew automatically. We send a reminder email before each renewal. Refunds for renewal charges are considered on a case-by-case basis — if you contact us within 48 hours of a renewal charge, we will typically issue a refund.

4. Annual Subscription Cancellation

If you cancel an annual subscription after the 14-day refund window, you will retain access to Pro features until the end of your paid period. No partial refunds are issued for unused months after the 14-day window.

6. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed through Paddle (our payment processor) back to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or card issuer — typically 5-10 business days after we approve the request.

7. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refund requests submitted more than 14 days after initial purchase
  • Accounts suspended for violations of our Terms of Service
  • Requests based on features that were clearly described before purchase
  • Subscription renewals requested more than 48 hours after the charge

8. Service Issues

If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact us immediately. We will work to resolve the issue or provide appropriate compensation, which may include extended subscription time or a refund at our discretion.
