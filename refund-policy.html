We want you to be satisfied. If you're not happy — we'll make it right.

1. Free Plan

The free plan is completely free — no payment, no refund needed. You can use 30 messages per day at no cost, forever.

2. Pro / Business / Ultra — 14-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to any paid plan on Marginova.AI and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

Conditions for refund eligibility:

  • Request submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date
  • Applies to first-time purchases of each plan tier
  • Account has not been suspended for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Refund request is not based on features clearly described before purchase

3. Subscription Renewals

Monthly and annual subscriptions renew automatically. We send a reminder email before each renewal.

If you contact us within 48 hours of a renewal charge, we will typically issue a full refund for that renewal. Requests after 48 hours are considered on a case-by-case basis.

4. Annual Subscription Cancellation

If you cancel an annual subscription after the 14-day refund window, you will retain access to paid features until the end of your paid period. No partial refunds are issued for unused months after the 14-day window has passed.

6. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed through Paddle (our payment processor) back to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or card issuer — typically 5–10 business days after we approve the request.

7. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refund requests submitted more than 14 days after initial purchase
  • Accounts suspended for violations of our Terms of Service
  • Requests based on features that were clearly described before purchase
  • Subscription renewals requested more than 48 hours after the charge

If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact us immediately at contact@marginova.ai. We will work to resolve the issue or provide appropriate compensation, which may include extended subscription time or a refund at our discretion.
